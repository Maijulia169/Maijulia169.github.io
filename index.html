FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Systeme.io really free forever?
Yes. The free plan supports up to 2,000 contacts, 3 sales funnels, 1 online course, and unlimited email sends — with no credit card required and no time limit. It's not a trial.
How does Systeme.io compare to ClickFunnels?
Systeme.io offers similar core funnel-building features at a fraction of the cost. ClickFunnels Basic starts at $97/month and doesn't include email marketing, courses, or affiliate management in lower tiers. Systeme.io includes all of these from $0/month. The trade-off is slightly fewer third-party integrations.
What payment methods does Systeme.io support?
Systeme.io integrates with Stripe, PayPal, Razorpay, Flutterwave, Xendit, Apple Pay, and MercadoPago — with 0% transaction fees on all plans.
Can I migrate from ClickFunnels to Systeme.io?
Yes. The Unlimited plan includes free migration assistance (valued at $1,997). For other plans, the Systeme.io support team can help guide the migration process.
Does Systeme.io have an affiliate program?
Yes — Systeme.io pays 60% lifetime recurring commissions to affiliates. Attribution is lifetime (not a 30-day cookie), so you earn every month your referral stays subscribed. The program is free to join with no application required.
Can I cancel anytime?
Absolutely. Cancel anytime from your account settings. Your data stays on the free plan as long as your contact count stays under 2,000. No lock-in contracts.
